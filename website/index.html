Thoughtfully Crafted

Every detail designed for writers who love markdown.

What You See Is What You Mean

No split view. No preview mode. Your markdown renders instantly as you type, creating a seamless writing flow that gets out of your way.

Tables Made Easy

Insert and edit tables with intuitive keyboard shortcuts. Add rows, columns, and navigate cells effortlessly.

Images Your Way

Drag & drop, paste from clipboard, or use the menu. Images auto-save to a tidy assets folder alongside your document.

Source Mode

Toggle to raw markdown with ⌘/ whenever you need to see exactly what's under the hood.

Document Outline

Navigate long documents instantly with the auto-generated heading outline in the sidebar.

Beautiful Themes

Switch between light and dark themes designed to be easy on the eyes during long writing sessions.

Keyboard-First Design

Every action at your fingertips. Stoodio MD is built for writers who think in shortcuts.
